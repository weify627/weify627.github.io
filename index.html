Fangyin Wei

fwei@princeton.edu

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science department at Princeton University. Prior to joining Princeton, I got my Bachelor's Degree in Intelligence Science and Technology from Peking University in 2018. My research interests lie broadly in computer vision and deep learning. I was a research intern at Sensetime Group Limited, Stanford University (advised by Prof. Leonidas Guibas), and Microsoft Research Asia.
