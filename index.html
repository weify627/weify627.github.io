Fangyin Wei

fwei@princeton.edu

I am a sixth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science department at Princeton University. I am very lucky to be co-advised by professor Szymon Rusinkiewicz and professor Thomas Funkhouser at Princeton. My research lies at the intersection of computer vision, computer graphics, and machine learning. I am currently interested in 3D modeling, synthesis, and editing through the lens of machine learning. I was a research intern at Reality Labs (Meta), Uber ATG R&D, Google Research, Microsoft Research Asia, and Stanford University. Prior to joining Princeton, I got my Bachelor's Degree in Intelligence Science and Technology from Peking University in 2018.
