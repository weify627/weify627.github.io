Fangyin Wei

fwei@princeton.edu

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science department at Princeton University. Now at Princeton, I feel super lucky to be co-advised by professor Szymon Rusinkiewicz and professor Thomas Funkhouser. Prior to joining Princeton, I got my Bachelor's Degree in Intelligence Science and Technology from Peking University in 2018. I am currently interested in visual and 3D understanding of the world through learning. I was a research intern at Reality Labs (Meta), Uber ATG, Google, Microsoft Research Asia, Stanford University (advised by professor Leonidas Guibas), and Sensetime Group Limited.
